Delectible ribs

  • Backstory
  • Ingredients
  • Method

Backstory

God used Adam's rib to make Eve, the first woman.
That was surely a worthwhile venture.
However, we think after trying this recipe, you'll realise the true answer to the question:
"What is the absolute best thing you can create using a rib."

Ingredients

  • Pork Ribs
  • Soy sauce
  • Honey
  • Chilli flakes

Method

  1. Marinate ribs in soy sauce for 48 horus
  2. Drizzle with honey
  3. Sprinkle over chilli flakes
  4. Bake at 180 for 90 minutes
